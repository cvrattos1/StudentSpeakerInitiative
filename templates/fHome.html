How It Works:

Right now students are nominating which conversation panels they would like to see on campus. These panels can range from anything between academic debate to popular culture discussions. However, before any of these panels can come to campus, they first need to be approved by a faculty member like you! If a panel doesn't have faculty approval, students won't even be able to vote for it in the final vote. By approving a conversation panel, you are giving your stamp of approval and allowing that panel to move on to the next stages of the cycle and helping students have a say in who they would like to see on campus.

For this cycle, you may approve 1 conversation, and you have until {{cycle.getDateEndorse().strftime("%b %d, %Y")}} to do so. Once a conversation is approved, the conversation will be able to move to the next steps of the cycle. If you would like to see the results of the final vote, they will be available on {{cycle.getDateResults().strftime('%b %d, %Y') }}.
